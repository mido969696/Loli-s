About Us

All over the world, people are being re-educated with regards to the benefits of using all things natural particularly products with direct contact to the human body with special emphasis on toiletries, beauty creams, and all other products related to personal grooming and the general well being of our skin, our hair and our entire body.

LOLI'S specializes in producing a line of 100% natural all handmade Egyptian accentuated body care products free from preservatives, chemicals, colors, and artificial fragrances.

The story

The company was started in 2023 by Alaa Atef, Alaa created her first natural body care product out of necessity.

It all began one years ago when the economic situation escalated in Egypt, all imported products were very rarely to be found. and the natural products are not common in Egyptian market, I searched for a natural products but could not find a single one with fair price. so, I decided to make it myself using natural ingredients. I made a pure olive oil soap and was surprised by the result. I luckily got it right the first time.

With a vision to increase the awareness of people regarding the harmful danger of chemicals in our lives and the benefits of chemical-free products, Alaa's passion for all things natural prompted her to start a cottage industry producing a small line of soaps, this line slowly expanded to include a multitude of natural products. The range of products is known as “Loli's”

The Concept

Loli's is a brand that believes in harnessing the power of nature to create natural and organic skin care products that are safe, effective, and sustainable. Our products are made with the purest ingredients sourced from nature, without any harmful chemicals or synthetic fragrances.

Our aim is to provide a holistic approach to skin care, by offering products that not only nourish and rejuvenate the skin but also promote overall well-being. We believe that what you put on your skin is just as important as what you put in your body, and that's why our products are made with ingredients that are safe enough to eat.

Our product line includes a range of skin care products such as cleansers, toners, moisturizers, masks, and serums, all made with natural and organic ingredients. We also offer customized skin care solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each customer.

At Loli's, we are committed to reducing our environmental footprint by using eco-friendly packaging and sourcing our ingredients from sustainable and ethical sources. We believe that by taking care of our skin with natural and organic products, we can also take care of the planet.

Join us on our mission to promote natural beauty, sustainability, and self-care with Lli's. Nourish your skin with the goodness of nature, and experience the difference for yourself.
